Tutorial Presenters

Tutor's are guaranteed the following benefits. Please note that while it is unlikely that full conference registration would not be available, refunds to help defer the tutor's travel and accommodation costs are contingent on the number of registrations for their tutorial as stated in the table below. The exact number of tutorial participants for each tutorial will not be known until the day of the tutorial. Refunds due will be made within a month of the close of the conference.


Duration Participants Tutor Benefits
Half-day (up to 4h duration) 7 to 14 —

15 to 24 1 conference registration

25 to 29 1 conference registration + up to US$650 refund of documented travel & accommodation expenses

> 29 1 conference registration + up to US$1,000 refund of documented travel & accommodation expenses
Full-day (up to 7h duration) 7 to 14 —

15 to 24 1 conference registration

25 to 29  1 conference registration + up to US$1,000 refund of documented travel & accommodation expenses

> 29  1 conference registration + up to US$1,350 refund of documented travel & accommodation expenses
